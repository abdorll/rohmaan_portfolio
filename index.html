About Me

I'm Opadeji Abdurrahman O., an Industrial Chemistry undergraduate based in Lagos, Nigeria. My journey began with a deep curiosity about the chemicals around us, which evolved into a passion for solving real-world problems—especially in the oil and gas sector, where Nigeria faces significant environmental and economic challenges.

Currently studying at Ekiti State University, I've complemented my academic work with hands-on lab experience and online certifications in Petroleum Engineering, Oil & Gas Management, and Gas Engineering. I'm also a member of professional student bodies like SCSN and NASS.

Driven to create cleaner, more efficient industrial processes, I'm actively seeking opportunities to apply my skills through internships and collaborative projects.
